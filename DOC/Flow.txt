 Overall System Flow Diagram

```text
[Creator / Consumer]
          |
          v
     [Web Browser]
          |
          v
   [React Frontend]
          |
          v
[Node.js / Express API]
      /             \
     /               \
    v                 v
[AWS S3]       [MongoDB Atlas]
(Photos)       (Users, Metadata,
                Comments, Ratings)
```

### 14.2 Photo Upload Flow Diagram

```text
[Creator Login]
       |
       v
[Open Upload Page]
       |
       v
[Select Photo + Enter Metadata]
       |
       v
[Frontend Sends Request]
       |
       v
[Backend Validates User + File]
       |
       +-------------------+
       |                   |
       v                   v
[Upload Photo to S3]   [Store Metadata in MongoDB]
       |                   |
       +---------+---------+
                 |
                 v
      [Send Success Response]
                 |
                 v
      [Display Uploaded Photo]
```

      [Frontend Displays Photos]
                   |
                   v
       [Consumer Opens Photo Page]
                   |

                   |
                   v

                   |
                   v

### 14.4 Search Flow Diagram

```text
[Consumer Enters Search Term]
              |
              v
   [Frontend Sends Search Request]
              |
              v
   [Backend Receives Search Query]
              |
              v
[MongoDB Searches Title / Location / Event]
              |
              v
     [Matching Results Returned]
              |
              v
   [Frontend Displays Search Results]
```

### 14.5 Authentication Flow Diagram

```text
[User Opens Login Page]
          |
          v
 [Enter Email + Password]
          |
          v
[Frontend Sends Login Request]
          |
          v
 [Backend Verifies User Data]
          |
          v
 [Generate JWT Token]
          |
          v
[Return Token to Frontend]
          |
          v
 [Store Token for Session]
          |
          v
[Grant Access Based on Role]
```
